Frequently Asked Questions

How much should I tip at a restaurant?

The standard tip at a restaurant is 15-20% of the pre-tax bill. For excellent service, 20-25% is appreciated. For average service, 15% is acceptable.

What is a 20% tip on $50?

A 20% tip on a $50 bill is $10, making your total $60. Use our tip calculator to instantly calculate any tip percentage on any bill amount.

How do I split a bill evenly?

Enter your total bill amount, select your tip percentage, then enter the number of people splitting the bill. The calculator will show each person's share including tip.

Should I tip on the pre-tax or post-tax amount?

Most etiquette experts recommend tipping on the pre-tax amount, though tipping on the total is also common. Our calculator lets you input either amount.

What is a good tip for delivery?

A standard tip for food delivery is 15-20% of the order total, or a minimum of $3-5 for small orders.
